About

Easteeend (pronounced East End) is a Western Canada-based filmmaker, director, and cinematographer. Helmed by U. Eddie Garcia R., Easteeend specializes in crafting visually compelling narratives across a diverse spectrum of mediums—spanning short films, intimate documentaries, striking music visuals, and high-energy live events. Driven by a passion for powerful storytelling and distinct imagery, Easteeend handles every stage of production from concept to lens, bringing a unique, collaborative vision to the vibrant landscape of Canadian filmmaking.
